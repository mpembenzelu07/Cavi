Featured Story

Jamila's Journey to Sight

At 8 years old, Jamila was living with congenital cataracts that severely limited her vision and ability to learn. Her family in rural Mwanza had lost hope.

Before Surgery

Jamila couldn't recognize faces or read. She depended on others for daily activities.

Surgical Intervention

CAVITRD's surgical camp in Mwanza performed successful cataract surgery in October 2022.

After Surgery

Jamila now attends school regularly, reads books, and dreams of becoming a teacher.
