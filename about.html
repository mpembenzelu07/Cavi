About CAVITRD

Learn about our mission, vision, and the team dedicated to transforming lives across Tanzania's Lake Zone region.

Our Story

Founded in 2017, CAVITRD (Children & Adults Vision Impairment & Trauma Related Disabilities) was established to address the critical need for specialized healthcare services in Tanzania's Lake Zone region.

Our journey began when our founders, Dr. Michael J. Mwita and a team of dedicated healthcare professionals, witnessed the devastating impact of untreated congenital anomalies and trauma-related disabilities in rural communities.

With registration number 14NGO/R/4896, we have grown from a small initiative to a recognized NGO making tangible differences in thousands of lives.

Join Our Journey

Mission & Vision

Our Vision

Provision of surgical treatment to children born with congenital anomalies and those who acquire trauma-related disabilities. We also facilitate necessary education to communities susceptible to certain congenital anomalies and support prevention by decreasing the incidence of trauma-related disabilities.

Our Mission

Provision of quality health care services and education to rural areas through organized surgical camps and outreach programs in the regions.

Our Objectives

  • Enhancing society's awareness of available treatment options
  • Advocating for prevention and protection measures
  • Providing surgical treatment to those in need
  • Supporting rehabilitation and reintegration

Our Core Values

Dignity

Equity

Transparency
