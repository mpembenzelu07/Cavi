Surgical Outreach Camps

Mobile surgical units bringing life-changing procedures to remote communities

Active Program

Transforming Lives Through Surgery

Our mobile surgical camps travel to the most remote areas of the Lake Zone, providing essential surgical interventions for congenital anomalies and trauma-related disabilities.

Each camp is staffed by experienced surgeons, anesthesiologists, and support staff, equipped with portable operating theaters and recovery facilities.

Services Provided:

  • Cleft lip and palate repairs
  • Burn contracture releases
  • Orthopedic corrections
  • Post-trauma reconstructions

Vision Care Services

Comprehensive eye care for preventable and treatable vision impairments

Restoring Sight, Transforming Lives

Our vision care program focuses on screening, diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation of vision impairments across all age groups.

We provide cataract surgeries, refractive error corrections, glaucoma management, and low vision support services.

Key Activities:

  • Community vision screening camps
  • Cataract surgical interventions
  • Eyeglass distribution programs
  • Vision health education
Ongoing Support

Community Education

Empowering communities through knowledge and prevention strategies

Awareness Building

Knowledge as Prevention

Our community education programs focus on raising awareness about congenital anomalies, trauma prevention, and available healthcare services.

We conduct workshops, training sessions, and awareness campaigns in schools, community centers, and through local media.

Education Topics:

  • Prenatal care and congenital anomaly prevention
  • Trauma prevention and first aid
  • Vision health and eye care
  • Disability rights and inclusion

Rehabilitation Services

Comprehensive support for recovery and reintegration

Journey to Recovery

Our rehabilitation program provides post-operative care, physical therapy, and psychosocial support to ensure complete recovery and successful community reintegration.

We work with local healthcare providers to establish sustainable follow-up care systems.

Services Include:

  • Physical therapy and mobility training
  • Psychosocial counseling
  • Vocational training
  • Community reintegration support
